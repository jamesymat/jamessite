Fur-ever Fat

Into the Mind of a Cat

Interviewer: Please introduce yourself.

Ragsy: Hi, my name is Ragsy. I am a ragdoll cat. We are known to be large, but I am a little bit bigger than the others. I have been on a diet for my whole life.

Interviewer: How old are you?

Ragsy: In human years, I am 9 years old and have been with my family for 8 years now.

Interviewer: What does a day in your life look like?

Ragsy: My life mainly consists of eating and sleeping.

Interviewer: What are your likes and dislikes?

Ragsy: I love food and people who feed me. I hate cars, loud noises, car rides, other animals, and having to move anywhere away from the food bowl.

Interviewer: What is your worst fear?

Ragsy: My worst nightmare is getting stuck in the cat door, again.

Interviewer: How do you get exercise?

Ragsy: I prefer to exercise by running to the food bowl or jumping onto the forbidden table.
